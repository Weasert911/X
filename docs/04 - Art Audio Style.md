# Art & Audio Style

## Visual Direction: Hand-Drawn Mature Stylized

### Core Style

- Hand-drawn, sketch-like outlines with visible pencil/texture quality
- Stylized but mature proportions (no chibi exaggeration)
- Imperfect lines for personality and organic feel
- Vibrant but grounded color palette (inspired by Indian street art)
- Balance between humor and visual sophistication

---

## Characters

- Medium scale with realistic proportions (approx 64x64 base)
- Expressive faces and body language for comedic timing
- Clear silhouettes for readability in chaotic scenes
- Clothing and details reflect Indian urban culture
- Animation retains humor but with more nuanced movement

---

## Environment

- Dense Indian streets (gali, markets, rooftops)
- Layered 2.5D look with depth
- Slight perspective tilt for dynamic feel
- Textured backgrounds with hand-painted details

### Key Elements

- Rickshaws, bikes, auto-rickshaws
- Small houses with accessible interiors
- Street props (crates, carts, water tanks, street vendors)
- Graffiti and wall art adding narrative layers

---

## Animation Style

- Snappy, exaggerated but controlled
- Squash & stretch used sparingly for impact
- Humor-driven motion with timing emphasis
- Fluid transitions between actions

---

## UI Style

- Hand-drawn icons with refined edges
- Slightly rough but clean aesthetic
- Minimal text, more intuitive symbols
- Color coding for quick recognition

---

## Audio Direction

### Voice Style

- Natural Indian slang (not forced memes)
- Short, punchy lines with comedic delivery
- Slight variations to avoid repetition
- Voice acting with mature but playful tone

---

### Sound Design

- Environmental chaos (crowds, dogs, horns, street sounds)
- Comedic timing for effects with subtle sophistication
- Clear audio cues for gameplay actions
- Layered audio for depth and immersion

---

## Key Principle

Clarity over detail.
Chaos must be readable at all times.
Humor through design, not through childish exaggeration.
